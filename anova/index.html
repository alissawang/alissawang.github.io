ANOVA

One-Way

How much of the difference between a point and the overall mean is explained by the group mean vs. random error?

The greater the difference coming from sample "error" compared to the difference between group and overall mean, the larger our treatment vs. error fraction is.

Sum of Squares Treatment (SST):

The squared difference between the overall mean and each group mean.

Sum of Squares Error (SSE):

The sum of squared differences between each sample point and its own group mean.
